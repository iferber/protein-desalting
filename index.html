Purification of azurin from total cell extracts

Step 7 & 8: Desalting

Lab supervisor: You have just obtained a 2.5mL sample from affinity chromatography. Desalting is the next step, to separate out azurin from contaminants. There are 4 main steps.

  1. Preparation of Desalting Column
    1. Remove the top cap and pour off the storage solution.
    2. Cut the sealed end of the column.
  2. Equilibration of Desalting Column
    1. Decide how many times to wash column with 25mL total equilibration buffer (Hint: The volume of the resin bed is 8.3mL)
    2. Pour equilibration buffer into column, allow to enter bed and drain.
    3. Discard the flow-through.
  3. Application of Cell Extracts
    1. Add 2.5mL sample to column using P1000 pipette.
    2. Decide whether to let sample enter bed fully or continue.
    3. Decide whether to keep or discard the flow-through.
  4. Elution of Azurin
    1. Add 3.5mL elution buffer to column using P1000 pipette.
    2. Collect eluate.
